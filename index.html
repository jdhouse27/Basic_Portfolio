I will have become the developer many want to employ.

In the Food and Beverage industry I have experience in all aspects, server, bartender, expoditor, cook, manager, general manager. My experience with web development has just begun, however throughout this website you will notice the many skills I currently have. Though my expertise may not be extremely advanced yet I live by a motto my grandfather taught me: "Lack of progression leads to regression". I am always looking for new growth and advancement.
